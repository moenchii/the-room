# the room

*turn on the lights.*

A 3-day setup for getting AI working inside your real workflow.
For solo-founders, producers, and creatives running multiple projects who want their own agent - as sharp as they are.

Day 1 - **your room.**
Set up your space. Cursor, Claude, Codex, the basics. By the end of day one, you have AI you can talk to about your project.

Day 2 - **source of truth.**
You tell the AI who you are, what you're working on, what your project is. Not generic. Yours.

Day 3 - **memory.**
MCP, agents that hold your work, a setup that remembers. By day three, you have something that doesn't just respond - it knows.

The room is structured but soft. You move at your own pace.

## Who this is for

You're a solo-founder, producer, or creative running your own thing - maybe one project, maybe five. You manage yourself, your work, your team if you have one. You want your own agent. As sharp as you, fluent in your context, not borrowed.

## What you walk out with

A setup that knows your project. The vocabulary to talk to it. The confidence to add a new tool next month without a 4-hour rabbit hole.

## What's in here

Right now: one command - `/warmup`. More will land here as the course grows.

`/warmup` walks any foggy task through six stages - ground → open → plan → brief → run → reflect - until you have a working first iteration. Type it in Claude Code, answer along.

## Install

Two ways. Pick what feels right.

**Terminal** (faster):

```bash
mkdir -p ~/.claude/commands
curl -L https://raw.githubusercontent.com/moenchii/the-room/main/commands/warmup.md -o ~/.claude/commands/warmup.md
```

**Manual** (slower, more visible):

1. Open [`commands/warmup.md`](commands/warmup.md) in this repo
2. Click **Download raw file** (↓ icon, top-right of the file content)
3. Move the file into `~/.claude/commands/` on your machine
4. In Claude Code, type `/warmup`
